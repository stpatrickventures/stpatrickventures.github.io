Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a career with St. Patrick?

As an equal opportunity employer, we are always looking to add effective and capable associates whose personal values align with ours to our team. St. Patrick Ventures is a family, and we treat our family well. We offer a variety of workplace initiatives, comprehensive and competitive benefits, and a great enviornment to grow and learn in. Visit our careers page for more information.

How do i form a partnership?

St. Patrick Ventures recognizes the importance of building strong working partnerships that can advance the quality of healthcare globally. Whether you are working on an innovative concept, are in search of a development partner, or are interested in exploring creative commercialization solutions for your company’s product, we can help you every step of the way. If you are interested in building a partnership with us, please send inquiries via our "contact" page, or feel free to email or call us.

How do I gain an investment?

Advancing the development of new healthcare solutions means collaboration. St. Patrick Ventures Innovation represents a new approach to partnering aimed at advancing early-stage innovation. St. Patrick Ventures Family of Companies teams of science and business experts, based in regional innovation centers, collaborates with innovators to accelerate cutting edge science into healthcare solutions. Let’s Get to Work on Your Next Project! Head over to our contact page for further inquiries.

*St. Patrick Ventures does not give unsolicited loans or funding.

*Serious inquiries only.

How do you help your community?

St. Patrick Ventures outreach activities include participation in trade fairs, service on supplier organization boards, and organization of its own internal opportunity fairs to directly connect its U.S. operating buyers to suppliers. We directly give back to our local communities by donating to health charities such as; St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, Operation Smile, and the Wounded Warrior Project.

How do i connect with your sales team?

Deep expertise and experience is what makes St. Patrick Healthcare Specialty Distribution & Manufacturing a proven leader in medical products custom-engineered to your specifications. Our seasoned team made up of engineers, material experts, skilled technicians, and support specialists are ready to be your single source solution for product development and production. Take and important step in getting your project off the drawing board and into the marketplace, head over to our contact page if you are interested in connecting with our team.

What is supplier diversity?

St. Patrick Ventures recognizes the importance of having a diverse supplier base that reflects it's patients and clients around the world. We are committed to working with small and diverse suppliers, who can support our long-term growth objectives and provide innovative solutions to marketing, manufacturing and R&D efforts. If you are interested in registering as a supplier with us, please visit our contact page.
